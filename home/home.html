WELCOME

The Dui-Chaka cycling club is an organization dedicated to encouraging the enjoyment of all types of cycling in the DCCC community. We welcome members of the DCCC community and friends to most of our club events such as group rides, classes, and community service. In addition to the club events DCCC cycling is the home of the four racing teams covering the four collegiate bicycle racing disciplines, Track, Road, Cyclocross, and Mountain. The team races in Divison II of the Cycling Conference (CC) organized by BD Cycling. If you have any questions or would like to join the club please contact us.
